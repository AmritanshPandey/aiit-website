Faculty Research Groups

Research Groups Functional Policy

In order to accelerate research group activities, the research groups are formed with following objectives:

  1. To promote collaborative research work and deliberate on research problems.
  2. To promote research groups for joint research paper writing, projects and books/chapters
  3. Share the research work and research experiences within and across the groups
  4. Patents & their commercialization out of research groups

Research groups are functional under overall guidance of Director, AIIT. Each research group contains a Group Coordinator/Co-coordinator, Faculty and research scholars as members. To achieve the goal group meetings used to conduct every month by the group coordinators/co-coordinators. AIIT contains five potential research groups namely Data Science, Image Processing& Computer Vision, Information and Network Security ,Software Engineering, division and Cloud Computing. Each research group headed by a Coordinator and a Co-coordinator.
