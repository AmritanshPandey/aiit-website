About Dr. BALVINDER SHUKLA

The Amity University is a leading private University in the country, having been accredited with ‘Grade-A’
by National Assessment and Accreditation Council, a statutory body of UGC.

I am delighted to note that Amity Institute of Information Technology (AIIT), an institute under Amity University, UP,
is taking an initiative to show case its achievements through a newly launched website under the domain of Amity University.

The information provided through the dedicated link of AIIT will help the students, faculty and all other stakeholders
to learn more about the academic and research activities of AIIT and the significant role it plays in providing ICT support
to other institutes of the University.

The students who are keen to develop their skill and competence in the field of IT would certainly take advantage
of this platform in pursuit of their academic excellence.

I offer my sincere compliments and best wishes to all the staff, faculty and students of AIIT and convey my whole
hearted support and cooperation in making AIIT a Centre of Excellence in the country.
