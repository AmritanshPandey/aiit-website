Cisco Regional Networking Academy and Instructor Training Center (ITC)

AIIT took the initiative to introduce CISCO Programme in the Amity Universe in the year of 2001 in collaboration with Cisco to bridge the gap of employable students in industry. Till now more than 1000 students have been passed out from AIIT Cisco Academy Support Centre (ASC) & Instructor Training Centre (ITC) and are well placed in industry. The courses are delivered in partnership with Cisco Networking Academy, a global education program that helps students prepare for ICT careers.

Since then AIIT has been the guiding light behind the development of CISCO programme in various Amity and other Institutions under this Regional Academy. The motive has always been to develop a High Quality Systems, Process & Standards in conformity with the best Institutes in India & World-wide. Through this Cisco Academy Program we are giving the opportunity of “Collaboration at the speed of innovation” and prepare our students to be industry ready. The main goal of academy is to create opportunities, space and scope for the development of the talents of the Indian youth.

Due to its continuous efforts in providing quality education in the field of Networking AIIT CISCO Networking Academy (RA) is recognized as an Cisco Academy Support Centre and Instructor Training Centre (ITC) by CISCO. This is an opportunity to enable our students to become a part of ready net force for jobs in the field of Network and Network Security. Portfolio of courses with Cisco Networking Academy IT Essentials, CCNA Routing & Switching, CCNA Security, Introduction to Cyber Security, NDG Linux, Internet of Things.

We have 76 academies currently associated with us across India and more than 1000 instructors have been given training from AIIT. More than 17000 students are currently registered with different academies under AIIT. AIIT Cisco ASC is helping Cisco Networking academies in more than 12 states to run the Cisco program effectively.
