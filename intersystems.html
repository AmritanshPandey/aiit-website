Intersystems Collaboration

  • The power of Post relational databases enables rapid development of various real time applications.

  • InterSystems Cache' is an advanced object databases that provides in memory speed with persistence, and the ability to handle huge volumes of transactional data.

  • In 2004, 18 June, Ritnand Baldev Education Foundation too initiative and signed a MoU with InterSystems Pvt. Ltd. USA to facilitate Cache' technology to Amity Students.

  • The aim of MoU was to train Amity Students in Cache' Technology in order to provide the IT market Cache' trained resources.

  • Amity Institute of Information Technology Cache' Lab with qualified trained faculty in Cache' technology has been teaching , guiding students and helping them in getting placed in companies like Birlasoft, 3einfotech, M.Tech India Pvt. Limited, Healthcare etc.. since 2004.

  • Amity students also get opportunity to participate on an International Platform "DEVCON" where they compete with international students for Cache' technology enabled projects. Since, 2004 more than 200 students have been trained in Cache' technology .

  • DEVCON is the call for students posting projects.
