Why AIIT?

Amity Institute of Information Technology (AIIT), Noida, which is the integral part of Amity University Uttar Pradesh, is a center of excellence for quality education in Information Technology with special focus on emerging trends.

AMITY UNIVERSITY VISION

Building the nation and the society through providing total, integrated and trans-cultural quality education and to be the global front runner in value education and nurturing talent in which Modernity Blends with Tradition.

AIIT MISSION

To provide education at:
  • Under Graduate
  • Post Graduate
  • Doctoral
  • in the field of Computer Science and Applications and in the futuristic and emerging areas of knowledge, Learning and research in the field of Information Communication Technologies.
To develop the overall personality of students by making them not only excellent Computing professionals and technocrats but also:
  • Good individuals
  • Understanding and regards of human values
  • Pride in their heritage and culture
  • A sense of right and wrong
  • Yearning of perfection and imbibe attributes of courage of conviction and action.
